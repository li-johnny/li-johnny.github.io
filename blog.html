Second Entry

  • What was challenging about the assignment, and what specific kinds of problems did you have. How did you solve those problems?
    • The filter was by far the most challenging part of the assignment, the countless hours of messing with position and display functions to make the input box and text inline with each other was traumatic. Eventually, I got on the right track and ended up using a flexbox to line everything up. Albeit, it took a trip to Frank for some CSS hours help, but I was on the right track and that's what really matters.
  • What did you learn from the assignment? Were there any special insights you had? What did you find that you already knew?
    • I learned that CSS requires heavy specificity in order to best manage everything. Alongside learning many new functions in CSS, recognizing which selectors to modify and the level of depth in their tree to modify was an insightful experience in applying my CSS knowledge. Prior to this, I had no insights or experience with CSS and everything I did, I was learning for the first time.
  • What kinds of resources were helpful for completing the assignment? Specific websites? Lectures? The class Piazza forum? The TAs? How did you use each of these resources?
    • I used a lot of W3school and went to Frank for office hours. While online resources gave a general guide/refresher to the function content, when it came to issue in applying that knowledge, I found TAs to be the more helpful correcting my knowledge. Many of the functions I used were learned on-hand and online in completing this assignment, and with TAs were two of the most helpful recourses for completing my assignment.
  • What are one or two things you had to Google to complete the assignment?
    • I googled almost every part of this assignment, many of the functions I used I had not remembered where or how to apply them, making Google a very helpful resource throughout this assignment. I spent a majority of the time googling how to make an input box and text, inline with each other before realizing display flex was the move. Then googling how to use drop shadows was one of the final parts of this assignment that soaked up a majority of my time.
